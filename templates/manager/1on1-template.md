## Anti-patterns we explicitly avoid

- ❌ Status updates (covered in standup / JIRA)
- ❌ Performance feedback ambushes (scheduled separately + previewed)
- ❌ Manager monologue (>50 % of agenda)
- ❌ Skipping when *manager* is busy (cancel only when IC has nothing)
- ❌ "Great chat" with no written follow-up
