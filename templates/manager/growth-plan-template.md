## Success criteria (binary — a peer manager could judge independently)

- [ ] <criterion 1 — e.g. "Framework PR shipped + retro with senior ICs">
- [ ] <criterion 2 — e.g. "Brown-bag delivered + recording + 5+ written attendees-take-aways">
- [ ] <criterion 3 — e.g. "L3 mentee has visible growth (their own growth plan ships)">

## Checkpoint cadence

- **Monthly review** in 1:1 (15 min, separate from regular topics) on the <day> of each month
- **Mid-point retro** at month 3 — re-scope if 1+ criteria are off-track
- **Promotion case** at month 6 — go/no-go via calibration with peer managers

## What this plan does NOT promise

> Critical clarity to set up-front: a growth plan is a *commitment to advocate*, not a *guarantee of promotion*.

- ❌ Hitting these criteria does not guarantee promotion (calibration with peer managers is required)
- ✅ Hitting these criteria **commits the manager to advocate** in the calibration
- ❌ Missing these criteria does not necessarily delay promotion (other evidence may exist)
- ✅ Missing these criteria triggers a written re-plan, not a quiet drop
