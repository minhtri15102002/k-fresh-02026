## How to read this doc

- **Currently does**: who does it today (often: you)
- **Will be done by**: the IC the work transfers to
- **Coaching plan**: 3-week handover (shadow → pair → solo)
- **Acceptable first-time failure**: what failure looks like that you will *not* punish (key for psychological safety)
- **Date target**: when the IC owns it solo, no manager safety net
